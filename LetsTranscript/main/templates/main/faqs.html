Frequently Asked Questions - Transcription - LetsTranscript

  • How much do you charge?

    LetsTranscript pricing starts from $0.80 per minute of audio. For instance, a 60 minute clip costs $48.
    There is no additional cost for multiple speakers, heavy-accent and there is no sales tax. All the recordings will be round up to the nearest whole minute.

  • How long does it take to deliver the transcripts?

    Files with good audio quality are delivered within the chosen turnaround time. For files with poor audio quality, it may take longer.

  • Do you transcribe multi-speaker recordings?

    Yes, we transcribe multi-speaker recordings and we charge the same price: $0.80 per minute. We label speakers appropriately "speaker 1", "speaker 2", etc.

  • Do you provide volume discounts?

    Yes, we offer volume discounts for qualifying customers. Contact our support team to learn more.

  • Do you offer transcription services in other languages?

    Yes, we offer automated transcription for more than 30 languages.

  • How do you keep information confidential?

    All our professionals have signed strict confidentiality agreements and the sensitive data of our clients is only disclosed in our secure platform.

  • Will LetsTranscript sign a non-disclosure agreement?

    We follow strict NDA norms to ensure confidentiality and data protection of our clients. This contract can be unilateral or mutual, we are happy to sign your NDA, or we can supply one for you.

  • How do you protect my files?

    We encrypt all data - both in transit and where it is stored on our protected Symantec servers. Your files are securely stored and transmitted using TLS 1.2 encryption consisting of bank-level security. To decode this idiom, it would take years to break this encryption and compromise our security.
    Our TLS encryption protocols fully guarantee the security of communications for all data exchanged.

  • How do you protect my payment information?

    We never store any credit card information - we simply pass it securely to our bank for safe-keeping. LetsTranscript maintains a PCI certification for payment processing.

  • Where can I find more information on LetsTranscript security and privacy policy?

    For more information please visit our Security and Privacy Page.
    Contact Us for any additional details and queries on security and privacy.
