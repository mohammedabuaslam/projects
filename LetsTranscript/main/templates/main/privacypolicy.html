Security of personal data


We comply with strict security procedures regarding the storage and disclosure of your personal data. The data provided is protected by SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology. SSL is the standard method used in the sector to encode personal information and credit card details in order to guarantee their secure transmission on the Internet.

All payment details are transmitted via SSL technology through the dedicated network infrastructure (Multiprotocol Label Switching - MPLS) and stored in accordance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards - PCI DSS.

Your information may be disclosed to trusted third parties for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We request that all third parties adopt adequate technical and operational security measures to ensure the protection of your personal data, in line with the GDPR regulations.

Cookies and web tracking


This site uses cookies to allow us to improve our services and to allow you to take advantage of some features of your interest. Advertising partner and media cookies stored on your device may be included when you visit our website. We invite you to visit our partners' website for information on their privacy and cookie policy.

Cookies are small text files transferred to your computer's hard disk from your web browser that allow us to recognize your browser and help us track visitors to our site, thus allowing us to identify the most suitable products and services to you. A cookie contains your contact information and information to allow us to identify your computer when you browse our site in order to help you complete your project booking. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but, if you wish, you can change your browser settings by accepting, rejecting or deleting cookies. The "help" section on the toolbar of most browsers will suggest you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies. If you choose to change these settings, some functions and features may not work properly. The cookies we use do not detect any information stored on your devices.

Your data protection rights

In certain circumstances, you have the legal right to:

• Request information to know if we keep personal information about you and, if so, to know what type of information it is and for what purposes we keep it and / or use it.

• Request access to your personal information (commonly known as "data subject's request for data access"). This allows you to receive a copy of the personal information that we keep on your account and to check that it is processed in accordance with current regulations.

• Request the rectification of personal information about you in our possession. In this way we have the possibility to correct any incomplete or inaccurate information.

• Request removal of your personal information. This allows you to request the deletion or removal of your information if there is no valid reason to continue using it. You also have the right to request the cancellation or removal by us of your personal information in the event that you have chosen to exercise your right of opposition. (compare points below)

• You object to the processing of your personal information in cases of legitimate interest (or in the legitimate interest of third parties) and in the event that your specific situation pushes you to object to the processing of your data on these bases. You also have the right to object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes.

• Opposition to automated decision-making tools including profiling, or not to be subjected to any automated decision-making tool by us through the use of your personal information or by carrying out profiling activities.

• Request the restriction of the processing of your personal information. This allows you to request that we suspend the processing of your personal information, for example if you wish to establish its accuracy or the reason for processing it.

• Request the transfer of your personal information in an electronic and structured format for you or another person (commonly known as data portability). This allows you to obtain the data we hold about you and to transfer it to an electronic format accessible also to a third party.

• Revoke consent - In the specific circumstances in which you have given consent for the collection, processing and transfer of your personal information for a specific purpose, you have the right to withdraw consent for this specific processing at any time. Once we receive the notification of your withdrawal of consent, we will cease processing your information for the purpose or purposes previously established, unless there are no further legal bases.

Access to your personal information (as well as the exercise of all other rights) does not involve the application of any fee. However, we may apply reasonable additional costs if your access request is clearly unfounded or excessive. We have the right to refuse your request if such circumstances exist.

We need to request specific information about you in order to confirm your identity and to guarantee your right to access information (as well as the exercise of all other rights.) This is an additional security measure aimed at ensuring that personal information is not disclosed to subjects who are not entitled to receive it.
